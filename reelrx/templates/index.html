What is ReelRx?

ReelRx is a machine learning powered app that provides quick movie recommendations for users based on a single input: Simply name a movie you enjoy!

The app utilizes data from the MovieLens 1M dataset. The dataset contain 1,000,209 anonymous ratings of approximately 3,900 movies made by 6,040 MovieLens users who joined MovieLens in 2000.

In conjunction with , ReelRx gets details like where to stream, rent, or buy movies it recommends.
