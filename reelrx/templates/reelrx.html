What is an "Rx Score"?

ReelRx uses state of the art machine learning algorithms to derive movie prescriptions you are likely to enjoy, given your input movie preference! The Rx Score is a nerdy way to quantify your prescriptions. The closer to one, the more confidence we have you will enjoy your Rx!
